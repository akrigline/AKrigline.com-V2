Greetings and Salutations

My name is Andrew Krigline

and I'm a designer

I am currently a junior studying Graphic Design at the University of Akron’s Myers School of Art. I've flown around the world seven times, been to nine countries, and have called two continents home. Ten years of my life were spent in China. I've interacted with people from all over the world and I believe that this experience has given me a more global perspective.

Right now I'm about to start interning at FlashStarts in Cleveland, Ohio. They're a company that accelerates the startup process with an intensive 12 week process. I'll be a design intern there and I'm very excited about the opportunity to meet the other interns and grow my professional network, as well as portfolio.

Most who know me would classify me as a ‘geek’, and I wear that title proudly. I taught myself how to code HTML and CSS when I was in 8th grade. Since then I’ve been fascinated with designing for the Web and more recently, mobile platforms. The technology, as well as its constant evolution has captured a great deal of my attention.

Beyond that I enjoy the classic works of J.R.R. Tolkien, C.S. Lewis, and Orson Scott Card, as well as the more recent Christopher Paolini and Stephen Lawhead. I consider myself a Trekkie, budding Whovian, and a Star Wars fan. Dabbling in 3D printing takes up my spare time and Coca-Cola is the fuel that feeds my creative engine.
